HOW TO START A TRANSPORT BUSINESS IN INDIA WITH LOW INVESTMENT

Dipesh Sagvekar
CEO & Founder, HK Transport

If you interested in running a transport business with minimum investment, we will share everything about this business with you & also share how much actual budget and documents are required to start a profitable transport business in India. There are three types of doing this business which are explained in detail below.

1. Owning A Truck

This type is also called as Truck Transport Business, which is also done by most of the people in India. Everyone thinks it is very easy to do as it only needs to buy a truck & then we have to give it on lease but let me tell you that it also has few risks to handle like sometimes the truck owner doesn't gets return load for the vehicle by which he has to bring it back empty & also cases like accidents but still as we know that every business has risks to deal with but still truck transportation business is a very profitable business to start if you have strong network. You can buy any truck from one small Tata Ace to big Tata Signa depends on your budget but before starting truck transportation business you should first look for manufacturing companies around your area becuase they are going to provide you the load for your truck to transport from one place to another. You should also decide that which route you are going to drive your truck. Owning a truck and giving it on a lease to a company with a contract is also the better way to start this business and earn more profit. If you are still confused about this topic, feel free to contact us. We will consult you for starting a truck transport business in India.

2. Starting A Booking Company

This type of business is best for people who has a budget of atleast 1 lakh and above. Booking company signs a contract with manufacturing companies to provide them truck for the specific period of time at a given price. The main thing they provide is to give credit of 1 month to those companies they had a contract. For Eg. A manufacturer has signed a contract with booking company to transport his material from Mumbai to Gujarat. Lets suppose the vehicle in this case is 17 feet truck & the trip cost is ₹20,000. So now the booking company will have to contact truck owners or brokers & get the vehicle below ₹20,000. Lets suppose booking company gets it for ₹17,000. Now booking company will assign the truck for manufacturer and in between the commission he earns is ₹3000. So if booking company gets 10 trucks orders monthly from the manufacturer then he will earn ₹30000 Per Month from this business. Only the thing in this case is booking company allows credit of one month to manufacturer which is why booking company needs more capital to start. If you are still confused about this topic, feel free to contact us. We will consult you for starting a truck transport business in India.

3. To Be A Broker Or Commission Agent

This type of business needs a budget of minimum ₹2000 to maximum ₹10000. This business is best for people who wants to start transport business in low investment. Even GST is not compulsory if you are starting as a broker or commission agent. The only work of broker is to have network of truck owners with him. Broker must have good relations with truck owners. There are two ways of earning for a broker. First, by connecting with booking company and providing them trucks and earning commission upto ₹1000 and second, by marketing his website on internet and getting direct customers. For eg. A customer contacts a broker to transport his goods of 300kgs from Mumbai to Bangalore and broker charges him ₹8000. Then broker will contact the truck owner whose route is Mumbai to Bangalore and have to assign the load below ₹8000. Lets suppose broker assigned it for ₹6000. Here he will earn ₹2000 commission in between. This business is so simple to do & some people in India are doing it without having an office. If you are still confused about this topic, feel free to contact us. We will consult you for starting a truck transport business in India.

HOW MUCH BUDGET IS REQUIRED TO START A TRANSPORT BUSINESS

Budget matters on which type of business you want to start. If you want to start truck transport business then the budget needed is the price of the truck. In this case office isn't needed compulsory. If you want to start a booking company then the minimum budget required is 1 lakh and above. If you want to start a brokerage business the you can start it with an initial investment of 2000 to 10000.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED TO START A TRANSPORT BUSINESS

Only GST is needed if you are starting booking business or truck transport business in India. Other documents like invoice is compulsory in this business.

HOW CAN I DO MARKETING OF MY BUSINESS

Marketing can be done in many ways like online advertisements to get direct customers and another way is to get email database of companies and sending them emails. There are many ways to do effective marketing of this business..

HOW CAN I FIND TRUCK OWNERS

In every city of India there is a transport association where a transport directory is published. You have to contact the association and get the book or you can also directly talk to truck drivers by meeting them.

IS TRANSPORT BUSINESS PROFITABLE

Yes, transport business is totally profitable if you have the right attitude of doing it. In transport business your communication skills also matters. Many people are doing this business and earning lakhs of rupees monthly and even brokers are earning rupees 40,000-50,000 monthly from this business. Transportation is the backbone of industry so this business will always grow.

TRANSPORT BUSINESS PLAN

Your business plan should have the name you want to give to your company, location of you business, the type of business you are starting. You should register for GST if you have more budget.
Your business plan may include :
  • A description of your finances.
  • A marketing strategy.
  • Names of members & manager including all contact information.
  • A plan of how you are going to deliver your services.
  • Defined goals for your business.
